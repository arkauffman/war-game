THIS IS WAR

Game Rules

Welcome, players. The rules are simple.

  • Both players starts off with 26 cards (half of a deck).
  • Each of you turn up the card at the top of your deck at the same time.
  • The player with the higher card takes both cards and puts them at the bottom of their stack.
  • If the cards happen to be the same rank, WAR is declared.
  • Each player must deal the top three cards on their stack faced down, with one card facing up.
  • The person that has the higher card takes the cards from WAR and adds them to their deck.
  • The winner holds the entire deck of cards in their power.

Best of luck to you both. Let the game begin.
